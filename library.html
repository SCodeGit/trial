E-Library Registration Process

To gain access to the Peki College of Education E-Library system, all students are required to complete registration before login credentials are issued.

Students may register using either of the following methods:
  • Physical registration at the School Library Office.
  • Online registration using the official Google Form below.

After submission, your details will be verified and entered into the E-Library system. Only approved students will receive login access.

Online Registration Form

Important Notice

After successful registration, please allow up to 24 hours for verification and approval. You will gain access using your Student ID and PIN provided during submission.

Once approved, access the E-Library here: www.pekicoelibrary.net
